Tools & Scripts

Facial Joint Rigging Tool [Python]

  • Tool uses user-created CV curves as guides for building the rig; this makes the tool very flexible and applicable to faces of any kind
  • Can build eyelid rigs and controls
  • Includes a function to store custom facial poses in the channel editor while allowing for individual controls to still be keyframed

Joint-Based Facial Rigging Tool

Biped Auto-Rigging and Pipeline Tool [Python]

  • Used to automatically generate a rig based on a template rig created by the tool
  • Can add squash and stretch to the arms, legs and spine during construction
  • Includes a function to prepare the rig created with this tool for export to game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine. Can also be applied to animation files.

Hi there, I'm David Chiu.

I grew up in Dallas, TX, where I earned both my undergraduate and graduate degrees in Arts and Technology at the University of Texas at Dallas. I can be described as a lover of puzzles and logic-based anything, whether it be in a game, part of the job, or just another instance of daily life.

I enjoy traveling to new places, especially to places that are relatively untouched by modern city development and/or are far away (distance-wise) from the familiar. I also like to play tabletop card games like Magic: the Gathering and watch videos of all sorts, ranging from documentaries to gameplay footage to independently made animations.
